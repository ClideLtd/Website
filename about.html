Who we are

Clide Technologies Limited is a Ghanaian-based firm with investments in Information and Communications Technology (ICT). Clide Technologies was set up in July 2011 by six young and vibrant Computer Scientist to revolutionize the state of Technology driven business in Ghana and the Sub-region. The name CLIDE is coined from the first letters of the ideals we stand for; Creativity, Leadership, Innovation, Determination and Entrepreneurship.


Our vision For the future

Our Vision is to be the main architect for the transformation of African Businesses by the transformation of Business technologies by the year 2020.
